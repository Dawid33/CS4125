Library Management System

Keep track of your books easily

Introduction:

This project was undertaken by Tomasz Zajas, Dawid Sobczak, Farran Haley, and John Walsh as their project for the module System Analysis and Design (CS4125), showcasing their collective expertise in creating a robust library management system tailored for academic institutions.

Innovative Cataloging and Book Management

Easily add, edit, and manage book inventories with unique identifiers. Multiple copies? No problem. Our system tracks each one meticulously.

Effortless User and Role Management

Our platform empowers students and faculty alike, with tailored borrowing limits. Administrators have full control to maintain a seamless user experience.

Seamless Search and Browsing Functionality

Dive into our expansive collection with intuitive search options. Find books by title, author, or even sort them by publication year. Detailed information is just a click away.

Automated Fine and Inventory Tracking

Never miss a due date again. Our system automatically manages fines for overdue books and keeps a clear record of your borrowed items.

Real-time Notifications

Stay informed with instant alerts for overdue books or when a reserved book becomes available, ensuring you're always in the loop.

Dedicated to Excellence

Our commitment to quality is unmatched. With a focus on the business tier of MVC architecture and implementation of design patterns like Observer and Factory Method, we ensure robust and scalable operations.
